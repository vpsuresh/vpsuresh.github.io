Sign Language Interpreting Services

Sign Language interpreting makes communication possible between people who are Deaf or Hard of Hearing and people who can hear. We interpret between English and American Sign Language (ASL), transliterate between spoken English and a form of English based sign that uses more word for word order and tactile interpreting used with the Deaf-Blind community.

Hayes Interpreting Services, LLC (HIS), is a reliable, professional and confidential sign language interpreting agency that provides that comprehensive communication.
